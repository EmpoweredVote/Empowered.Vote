Putting people before parties

We are building a civic platform that empowers Citizens in the Information Age. It’s a broad solution, so we focus our features across a few distinct vectors:

Citizens

If a democracy is going to be guided by its Citizens, then Citizens must participate in the process. We leverage game development techniques to make healthy civic participation as engaging as possible.

Speakers

For those looking to interact with others, we’ve created a series of small, connected communities. Each is actively designed to be productive, leveraging mediation techniques developed in the behavioral sciences.

It’s not Me versus You.
It’s Us versus a Shared Problem.

Leaders

Candidates must raise tens of millions to mount an effective electoral campaign. When we require wealth to get elected, then our democracy will primarily serve the wealthy.

Why Shared Solutions?

There are three reasons we focus on Shared Solutions:

A solution sincerely shared lasts for a very long time without requiring any manipulation or coercion to remain in place.
Shared Solutions are a prerequisite for Democracy, World Peace, and the Golden Rule.
Committing to the pursuit of Shared Solutions gives us a framework to highlight when those among us do not belong.
