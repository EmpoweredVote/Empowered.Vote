Why Shared Solutions?

Shared Solutions last for a very long time without any need for manipulation or coercion to remain in place.
Shared Solutions are necessary for Democracy to function. It’s why ‘United’ comes before ‘States’.
Shared Solutions create guardrails. No one will ever need to lie to you to get you to embrace a shared solution.

How It Works
